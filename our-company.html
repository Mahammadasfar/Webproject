Our Company
One of Abu Dhabi’s Leading Family Businesses

Established in 1970, Al Masaood Group is one of Abu Dhabi’s highly diversified family business that operates 21 global brands

Since its inception, the Group has been contributing to the success story, economic growth, and diversity of the region. With three main verticals - Mobility, Industrial, and Property - Al Masaood is a trusted partner for both public and private sectors in Abu Dhabi.

Aligned with the UAE's strategy for sustainable growth and economic diversity, Al Masaood provides a wide range of industrial solutions across strategic sectors, including utilities, marine, heavy equipment & machinery, logistics, and modular construction. It also caters to various retail sectors, such as automotive and accessories.
