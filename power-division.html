Al Masaood Power Division
Established in 1972, the Al Masaood Power Division is a global leader in Diesel and Gas Power Generation Systems.

Specializing in systems ranging from 94 KVA to 3,390 KVA, the Division represents MTU (a Rolls-Royce Power Solutions Company), Volvo Penta, and Nidec Leroy-Somer in the UAE and Bahrain. Known for its top- tier sales and aftersales services, the professional team offers cutting-edge, customized, and efficient solutions. The Division also partners with key industry leaders like ADWEA, DEWA, FEWA, and SEWA.
